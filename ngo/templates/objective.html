Our Objective - Bandiramchar Development Foundation

Bandiramchar Development Foundation is a community-based organization in Santhia, Pabna dedicated to creating positive change in the lives of underprivileged children, their families, and communities. The organization focuses on promoting welfare activities, supporting people with disabilities, and assisting the rural poor. It also utilizes impact monitoring to enhance project management and effectiveness.

  • Provide emergency healthcare assistance in rural areas.
  • Facilitate scholarships and sponsorships for higher education.
  • Build networks of self-help groups for women and youth.
  • Reduce rural-to-urban migration by creating local employment opportunities.
  • Monitor and evaluate program impact to ensure sustainable development.
